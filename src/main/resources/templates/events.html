• 504LIFE Telethon
    I served as Partnerships Manager for 504LIFE - a volunteer group who organized a series of online telethons to raise funds for first responders, medical workers and health organizations in Louisiana. I initiated and managed partnerships with fifteen of New Orleans’ iconic music venues and a series of other non-profit organizations, local interest groups, Mardi Gras Krewes and more. I designed a cross-posting marketing strategy to drive traffic from each partner organization’s Facebook community. I also built the 504LIFE website. 504LIFE events raised more than $25,000, and our platform (Sofa King Fest) went on to raise an additional $100,000 for COVID relief.
  • Memorial Day Meltdown
    Memorial Day Meltdown was a 12-hour livestream concert on Memorial Day 2020 that I executive produced in partnership with Goner Records, Burger Records, Slovenly Recordings and Sofa King Fest. I coordinated marketing efforts with the labels and the forty bands who participated, and I used Open Broadcaster Software (OBS) to produce the show using a combination of pre-recorded videos and livestreams from Chile, Japan, Europe and the U.S. Using cross-posting and cross-platform marketing, I generated 50,000 unique views and 8,000 new users on the Sofa King Fest platform.
  • Memphis on the Move
    As a Sales & Marketing Associate at Launch Pad, a national co-working startup, one of my many duties was to generate leads and drum up interest in our new spaces. My initial efforts focused on Launch Pad Memphis. In my first month, I developed Memphis on the Move (MOTM), a monthly event series that encourages the community to connect and learn from leaders who are moving Memphis forward. Between the first and second MOTM events, I revamped Launch Pad’s marketing & outreach strategy resulting in a 300% increase in RSVPs and attendance. Unfortunately, Launch Pad Memphis has since closed due to the COVID-19 pandemic.
